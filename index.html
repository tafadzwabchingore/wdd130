Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country in southern Africa known for its dramatic landscape and diverse wildlife, much of it within parks, reserves and safari areas. The name Zimbabwe is derived from the Shona words, dzimba dzemabwe, meaning houses of stone or stone buildings, symbolizing the Great Zimbabwe Ruins near the present day town of Masvingo.
