Overview

Purpose

We offer the paddle of a lifetime down the Zambezi River on a 14-mile (23-kilometer) whitewater rafting course from Victoria Falls. A highly professional and experienced rafting guide will steer you on a memorable ride through Class IV and V rapids in a gorge reaching depths of 650 feet (198 meters) in the heart of Zimbabwe's wilderness. Your tour includes lunch, open bar, and round-trip hotel transport.

Audience

Are you looking for some heart thumbing activities? Are you a parent interested in taking your children though the adventure of a lifetime? Do you want to partake in a safe but affordable water rafting tour? Well you have come to the right place. At White Water Rafting we offer safe a safe white water rafting experience with well trained and experienced rafting guides to guide you through a ride full of adrenaline rush. We have no age restrictions so children are welcome however they must weighs at least 50 lbs (23 kg), fits in a Class 5 youth life jacket, and has a parent's permission; if weight is less than 50 lbs, he or she can only do Level 3 rapids and must skip the Level 5 rapids this will also depend on the guide's decision on which rapids or sections your child can do.
